Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

Short version: InkCraft Studios collects only the information we genuinely need to deliver our services, communicate with you, and improve our website. We never sell your data to third parties, and we store what we collect securely.

1. Who We Are

InkCraft Studios ("we", "us", "our") is a content writing agency operating under Indian law. Our registered address is available on request. We are the data controller for any personal information collected through this website and through our service delivery process.

If you have any questions about this policy or the way we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@inkcraftstudios.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information in the following ways:

  • Contact forms: When you submit a quote request or get-in-touch form, we collect your name, email address, phone number (if provided), company name, and the content of your message.
  • Email correspondence: When you email us directly, we retain that correspondence and the personal data it contains for the purpose of managing your enquiry or project.
  • Project briefs: As part of delivering our services, you may share business-specific information, brand guidelines, or confidential documents. This information is used exclusively to complete your project.
  • Payments: We do not store payment card details ourselves. All payment processing is handled by third-party providers (Razorpay, Stripe, or PayPal) who operate under their own privacy and security standards.
  • Website analytics: We use anonymous, aggregated analytics to understand how visitors use our site — including which pages are viewed most and where traffic comes from. This data does not identify individual users.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotations for our services.
  • To deliver the content writing and SEO services you have contracted us for.
  • To send you project updates, draft deliverables, invoices, and relevant communications.
  • To improve our website content and user experience through anonymous analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including tax and accounting requirements.

We do not use your personal data to send unsolicited marketing emails. If you have opted into our newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link provided in every email.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing is necessary to fulfil a contract with you or to take steps you request before entering into a contract.
  • Legitimate interests: We have a legitimate interest in responding to enquiries and improving our business, which does not override your rights.
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required by law to process or retain certain data.
  • Consent: For optional data collection (such as newsletter sign-ups), we rely on your explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party tools for email (Google Workspace), project management, and payment processing. These providers act as data processors and are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Writers and contractors: To complete your project, a member of our writing team will access your brief and related materials. All team members and contractors are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal processes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy. Specifically:

  • Enquiry and contact form data is retained for up to 2 years from the date of last contact.
  • Client project files and correspondence are retained for 5 years from the date of project completion for legal and accounting purposes.
  • Payment records are retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law.

After the applicable retention period, we securely delete or anonymise your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records.
  • Objection: You may object to our processing of your data where we rely on legitimate interests as our legal basis.
  • Portability: You may request that we provide your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@inkcraftstudios.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption on all web pages and data transmissions.
  • Password-protected systems with restricted access to client data.
  • Regular security reviews of our tools and processes.
  • Confidentiality agreements with all team members and contractors.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to take reasonable precautions when sharing sensitive information online.

9. Cookies

Our website uses a small number of cookies to improve your experience. These include:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous data that helps us understand how visitors use our site. No personal information is collected through these cookies.

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use our website.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
