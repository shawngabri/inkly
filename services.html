Yes, absolutely. Every piece of content that leaves our studio is written by a trained human writer. We do not use ChatGPT, Jasper, or any other AI generation tool in our writing process. We rely on original research, industry reading, and craft. Our clients pay for genuine human expertise — and that is precisely what they receive.

Standard turnaround for most pieces is 48 to 72 hours from the moment we receive your brief and any reference materials. Growth and Authority clients receive priority 24-hour turnaround. For urgent same-day requests, please reach out directly and we will do our best to accommodate.

A good brief covers: the topic or goal of the piece, your target audience, your preferred tone (professional, conversational, bold, etc.), any keywords you want included, competitor links or examples you admire, and the word count you need. We also send you a short questionnaire when you sign up to make sure nothing is missed.

Growth and Authority clients get unlimited revisions — we revise until you are genuinely happy. Starter clients get two rounds. If after two rounds you still feel something is off, reach out and we will sort it; we do not want any client walking away unsatisfied with their content.
