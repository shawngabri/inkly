Refund & Revision Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

Our commitment: We stand behind the quality of every piece of content we produce. If we fall short of the agreed brief, we will make it right — through revisions, partial credits, or a full refund where appropriate. Our goal is for every client to feel genuinely satisfied with their investment.

1. Our Revision-First Approach

Before a refund is considered, we always attempt to resolve any quality concern through revisions. Writing is a collaborative process, and the first draft is rarely the final word. We strongly encourage all clients to request revisions before escalating to a refund claim, because in the vast majority of cases, a clear round of feedback is all it takes to get to a result that everyone is proud of.

Revisions are unlimited for Growth and Authority clients, and two rounds are included for Starter clients. There is no charge for revisions that fall within the original agreed scope of work.

2. What Qualifies for a Refund

A refund may be issued, in whole or in part, under the following circumstances:

  • We significantly miss the brief: The delivered content does not match the agreed topic, word count, format, tone, or key requirements outlined in your written brief — and repeated revisions have not resolved the issue.
  • Confirmed plagiarism: If content we deliver contains plagiarised passages not detected in our own pre-delivery Copyscape check, you are entitled to a full refund of the affected pieces.
  • Significant delay caused by us: If we miss a confirmed deadline by more than 5 business days without prior notice or a mutually agreed extension, you may be entitled to a partial or full refund for the delayed work.
  • Service not commenced: If you have paid in advance and we have not yet started work on your project, you are entitled to a full refund of the amount paid.
  • Duplicate charge: If a payment error results in you being charged more than once for the same order, the duplicate payment will be refunded in full within 3 business days of our being notified.

3. What Does Not Qualify for a Refund

The following situations are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Change of mind: If you decide you no longer need the content after work has commenced, we cannot issue a refund for work already completed. In these cases, a credit note may be offered at our discretion.
  • Subjective dissatisfaction after revisions: If we have delivered content that matches the original brief and completed the agreed number of revision rounds, but you remain dissatisfied due to subjective personal preference rather than a specific, articulable issue, a refund will not be issued. This is why we encourage detailed briefs and open communication throughout the process.
  • Changed requirements after delivery: If the brief changes significantly after work has been completed (for example, a new brand voice, a completely different angle, or a different target audience), the additional work will be treated as a new order rather than grounds for a refund.
  • Third-party performance: We cannot refund based on the performance of content on search engines, social media, or advertising platforms, as these outcomes depend on many factors outside our control, including your website's domain authority, technical SEO, and promotional strategy.
  • Downloadable or published content: Once content has been published on a live website or distributed publicly, refunds are not available for that specific piece. Please review all content thoroughly before publishing.

4. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to billing@inkcraftstudios.com with the subject line: Refund Request – [Your Name / Order Reference]
  • Include your name, the date of payment, the amount paid, your invoice or order reference number, and a clear explanation of the issue.
  • If your concern is quality-related, please attach the delivered content and indicate specifically where the brief was not met.

We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days and aim to resolve all refund disputes within 10 business days. During this period, we may request additional information or propose an alternative resolution such as a credit note or replacement content.

5. Refund Processing & Timelines

Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method:

  • Bank transfer / UPI / Razorpay: 5 to 7 business days after approval.
  • PayPal: 3 to 5 business days after approval.
  • Stripe / Credit Card: 7 to 10 business days after approval, depending on your bank or card issuer.

If you do not see the refund within the stated timeframe, please check with your bank first before contacting us, as processing times can occasionally vary.

6. Monthly Retainer Cancellations

For clients on monthly retainer packages (Starter, Growth, or Authority):

  • You may cancel your retainer at any time by giving us 14 days' written notice via email.
  • Work that has already been delivered within the current billing cycle is non-refundable.
  • Work that has been paid for but not yet commenced at the time of cancellation will be refunded on a pro-rata basis.
  • There are no cancellation fees or penalties — we believe in making it easy to leave, because we would rather earn your continued business through the quality of our work.

7. Disputes

We genuinely hope that every client relationship ends positively, whether that means a long partnership or an amicable parting. If you are unhappy with the outcome of your refund request, please escalate to our Founder directly at aryan@inkcraftstudios.com. We will always listen and do our best to find a fair resolution.

In the event of an unresolvable dispute, the matter will be governed by Indian law and subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in India.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. The policy in force at the time of your payment is the one that applies to your order.
